Escape Tarkov Together

Tired of constantly being shredded? Need some help from experienced players? If you said yes to any of those then you're in the right place.

Match up with High Ranking Good Players in your Timezone!

Escape Tarkov Together was developed by four friends who became really good at the game, and started to help new players learn the game while playing with them.

Since we have grown since then, you will be automatically matched with someone in your timezone and he or she will help you learn the game and help you get extracted and make money!

It works in 3 easy steps

  1. Sign up for our service.
  2. After that you will be automatically matched with someone from your Timezone whom is ready to play with you.
  3. Add each other then escape!
