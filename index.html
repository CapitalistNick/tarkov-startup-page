Nick Laurenzo's Career

Nick Laurenzo, first started politics in December of 2017, his first job was Governor of New York, after some time he started to grow in popularity. People sees Nick as Bipartisan, fair, honest man, who stands up for his beliefs.

President Issa, appointed Nick Laurenzo as Secretary of Homeland Security, after serving a while in that Mr. Laurenzo became the Midwestern Senator at which he held Minority Whip. After that Mr. Laurenzo started his career in the House of Representatives.

His first term he was the Representative for District 4. After that he became the Representative for District 9, where he held Deputy Leader then at the end of the term Speaker Fareo appointet Mr. Laurenzo as his Speaker Pro Tempore. Now Mr. Laurenzo is currently the Representative for District 2 which is his homestate of New York and he couldn't be prouder.
